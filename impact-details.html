June 18

College PREP Program

Lagos, Nigeria

On June 18, the African Youth Baseball & Softball Empowerment Foundation (AYBSEF) hosted an inspiring "College PREP Program" in Lagos, Nigeria. This event aimed to equip young athletes with the necessary skills and knowledge to pursue higher education opportunities, both locally and internationally.

Held in a vibrant and energetic atmosphere, the program featured a series of workshops and sessions focused on academic readiness, college application processes, scholarship opportunities, and the importance of balancing sports with academics. Participants had the chance to interact with educational consultants, former student-athletes, and successful professionals who shared their experiences and provided valuable insights. The event was a remarkable success, with enthusiastic participation from aspiring young baseball and softball players.

A few photos captured during the event showcase the engagement and excitement of the attendees, highlighting their commitment to personal and academic growth.

AYBSEF continues to empower youth through sports and education, fostering a brighter future for the next generation of athletes and scholars.
