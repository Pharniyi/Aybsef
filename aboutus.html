About Us

The African Youth Baseball & Softball Empowerment Foundation (AYBSEF) is an unbiased, non-profit, and non-governmental foundation committed to creating a stimulating and supportive environment to increase the participation of youths in Africa in baseball and softball. The foundation, registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, Nigeria, seeks to act as a development agent to encourage participation in the games of Baseball, Softball and other sports that can ultimately empower youths in Africa through structured long-term athlete development programs driven by robust technical development programs.

Vision

To position Nigeria/Africa as a leading nation in Baseball and Softball by developing world-class athletes and providing access to high-quality facilities and training programs.

Mission

To create a vibrant and sustainable Baseball and Softball ecosystem in Nigeria/Africa by providing quality facilities, training programs, and fostering talent to cultivate world-class athletes.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We believe in the power of innovation, and are committed to exploring new ideas, approaches, and technologies to improve the quality of our program and service delivery continually.

Collaboration

Baseball and Softball development requires a collective effort, and we are committed to collaborating with stakeholders, partners, and communities to achieve our goals.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in developing world-class athletes and coaches, and in providing high-quality facilities and training programs.

Integrity

We are committed to operating with integrity, honesty, and transparency, and we hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards.

Respect

We value the contributions of all our stakeholders and treat them with respect, dignity, and professionalism.

Sustainability

We are committed to promoting sustainable practices in all aspects of our operations, including the development of environmentally friendly facilities and programs that impact our communities.

Inclusivity

We believe in the strength in diversity and inclusivity, and are committed to providing opportunities to all regardless of background, gender, or socio-economic status.
